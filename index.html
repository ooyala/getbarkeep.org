Who is the Barkeep?

The Barkeep is a fast, fun way to review code. Engineering organizations can use it to keep the quality bar high. With it you can watch commits made to any Git repository, see diffs, write comments, and have those comments emailed to your fellow committers.

How he does it

Syntax-highlighted colored diffs

They're easy on the eyes.

Side-by-side or unified diff views

Different strokes for different folks.

Threadable comment emails

Subscribe to just comments, commits, or both. The emails are clean and plaintext friendly.

Excellent keyboard shortcuts

No mouse required or your money back. Just type "?" on any page to see a list of shortcuts for that page.

A good UI

If you use it every day it might as well be pleasant.
